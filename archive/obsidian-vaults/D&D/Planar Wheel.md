Consists a hub and 4 axes: 
* Axies of Dream 
	* [[Feywild]] = the Dream
	* [[Shadowfall]] = the Nightmare
* Axis of Reality
	* [[Material Plane]] = the Physical
	* [[Far Realm]] = the Mental
* Axis of Time
	* [[Positive Energy Plane]] = the Future
	* [[Negative Energy Plane]] = the Past
* Axis of Meaning
	* [[Heaven]] = the Good
	* [[Hell]] = the Evil
